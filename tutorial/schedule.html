This schedule starts from when the tutorial ends and you have control over Joker.

There is wiggle room if you don't stick to the schedule exactly, so don't worry too hard about it. The most important thing is to not forget to save oftenly in case there are mistakes.

Make sure to always capture the Treasure Demons while in the palace.
