# Game Hijack
### Christina Chueng
When viewing a game website to look for a game, one of them doesn't seem quite right. It appears to be hijacked by a fashion group.<br>
<br>
In the late 20th and early 21st centuries, Japan had an economic crash. Stocks and employment rates were at record lows, and the ones suffering the most from it were the young university students and graduates who were expected to enter the workforce. Most of them weren't able to land decent jobs and are still affected by this today, this group of people being known as the "lost decade". 
<br>
People in this era turned to other ways for self-expression and to quietly rebel against the system that failed them, one of them being fashion. I used a popular game company in Japan's websites to create a narrative where a popular comic and show from the same era came out, representing the fashion industry at the time. 
<br>
The main characters of the story run a fashion group named "Paradise Kiss," and this is an interpretation of if they were to hijack this game company's website.
