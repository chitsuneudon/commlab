Introduction

The Lost Decade, economic crash, and the overwhelming presure to conform to expectations. Unemployment and debts at record lows and no government action.

The main characters must turn to other ways to challenge traditional societal norms and standards in order to quietly rebel against stagnation.

Using fashion...?

  • Characters

    A fashion collective

    preparing for thier fashion showcase

    to rebel against society.

  • Yukari "Caroline" Hayasaka

    A high-school senior pressured to follow a traditional path forced onto her by her mother. She discovers her love for fashion and wishes to become a model, rebelling against her mother in order to follow her dreams.

  • Joji "George" Koizumi

    The leader of the Paradise Kiss group. A charismatic but rude fashion designer who pushes those around him to discover their passions and follow their dreams.

  • Miwako Sakurada

    A member of the ParaKiss group often coming across as naive and immature and easily taken advantage of. However her one true love is fashion, and she slowly understands her own emotions.

  • Arashi Nagase

    A hot tempered member of the ParaKiss group who cares deeply about those within his inner circle. He is the punk rockstar of the group and struggles with insecurities.
